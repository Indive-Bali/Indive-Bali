EAST NUSA PENIDA TOUR

Nusa Penida is one of Bali's most beautiful islands that offers a truly memorable vacation experience. Located alongside Nusa Ceningan and Nusa Lembongan, East Nusa Penida features a unique charm with stunning coastal views and hidden gems.

In the East Nusa Penida Tour, there are several must-visit destinations that will take you through some of the most beautiful landscapes on the island, such as Atuh Beach, Diamond Beach, and the famous Tree House Nusa Penida.

Atuh Beach

Atuh Beach is known for its white sandy shores and dramatic cliffside views. It's an ideal location for swimming, snorkeling, and relaxing on the beach. The scenic hills surrounding the beach offer stunning panoramic views.

Diamond Beach

Next to Atuh Beach, Diamond Beach is known for its pristine white sand and crystal-clear water. With its iconic coconut tree swings and impressive cliffs, it's one of the most photographed spots in Nusa Penida.

Tree House Nusa Penida

The Tree House Nusa Penida offers breathtaking views of Atuh Beach from a perched vantage point. A truly unique experience, it provides the perfect photo opportunity with stunning surroundings.
