EAST BALI TOUR

East Bali Tour is a Bali Full Day Tour Package designed to visit various attractions in the eastern part of Bali. This one-day tour takes you to explore the beautiful natural panoramas of East Bali. Along the way, you'll be guided by an experienced and friendly driver, ensuring that the trip is both enjoyable and memorable.

The tour starts with a visit to Taman Ujung Water Palace, a beautiful park created by the King of Karangasem as a place to relax, followed by a visit to Tenganan Village, an ancient village known for its traditional customs and the art of Pegringsingan weaving. After that, enjoy a delicious lunch before continuing the journey to more stunning attractions.

Lempuyang Luhur Temple

The Penataran Agung Lempuyang Temple is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Karangasem. With stunning natural views, this temple is an Instagram-worthy spot. Many tourists visit this location for the famous "Gateway to Heaven" photo opportunity.

Tirta Gangga Water Palace

Located in Karangasem, Tirta Gangga Water Palace is a beautiful water recreation park with well-maintained gardens and pools. It's an ideal destination for a peaceful escape and a must-see in East Bali.

Tukad Cepung Waterfall

Tukad Cepung is a hidden waterfall in Bangli that has become a popular tourist spot for its unique and picturesque setting. The waterfall is surrounded by towering cliffs, creating a magical scene.

Besakih Temple

Known as Bali's Mother Temple, Besakih Temple is a sacred site located on the slopes of Mount Agung. It's a popular destination for both religious pilgrims and tourists who want to experience the spiritual heart of Bali.
