NORTH BALI TOUR

If you ask, where is the center of art and culture in Bali? Many people would say Ubud. Not only famous for its arts and culture, Ubud also offers the natural beauty of the countryside, which is well-preserved and serene.

Besides its art and culture, Ubud is also famous for its shopping centers, offering everything from souvenirs to branded goods. Ubud is a perfect spot for culinary tourism, with many restaurants offering Balinese delicacies and international cuisines.

Ulun Danu Temple

Ulun Danu Temple is located on the edge of Lake Beratan in Bedugul, Bali. It’s one of Bali’s most iconic temples, offering stunning views of the lake and surrounding mountains. This is a must-visit destination for nature and culture lovers.

Jatiluwih Rice Terraces

Jatiluwih is a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its breathtaking rice terrace fields in Tabanan Regency. If you’re looking for a peaceful and natural rural atmosphere, Jatiluwih is the perfect destination to enjoy Bali’s traditional agricultural landscape.

Candi Kuning

Candi Kuning is located near Bedugul in Tabanan and is famous for its proximity to the Ulun Danu Temple. The area offers a mix of cultural significance and beautiful landscapes, making it a popular stop for visitors heading to the temple.

Handara Gate

The Handara Gate is located near the Handara Golf & Resort Bali. Known for its iconic Balinese gate surrounded by lush greenery, this spot offers a picturesque and Instagram-worthy scene, making it one of North Bali's most famous landmarks.
