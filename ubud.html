UBUD TOUR

As Bali known with its art and culture, then you will ask, where is the center of art and culture in Bali? Surely most people who have known Bali will answer, Ubud. Not only art and culture, Ubud also offers natural beauty of the countryside that is still natural and very well preserved.

Besides being famous for its arts and culture center, Ubud is also famous for its shopping centers, both souvenirs and branded goods. Not only handicrafts and souvenir shops, in Ubud destination, there are many places to eat from various countries. For those who like Balinese culinary tourism, Ubud provides many dining facilities, ranging from simple stalls to luxury restaurants.

Tegalalang Rice Terrace

Tegalalang rice terraces destination in Ubud is very popular with Indonesian tourists. It is because the location of Tegalalang rice terraces in Ubud is in the same direction as the Kintamani destination. The view of the Tegalalang rice terraces in Ubud is very unique. It has a slope of terraced rice fields of about 40 degrees. When visiting the Tegalalang rice terrace, Indonesian tourists will take photos and hang out at one of the Tegalalang cafes.

Monkey Forest Ubud

Monkey Forest Ubud is one of must-visited destination in Ubud. It is because Monkey Forest in Ubud is very iconic and represents the impression of the Ubud destination as a whole. In addition, the Ubud monkey forest is a protected forest area and in this forest, there is a sacred Balinese Hindu temple, named Pura Dalem Agung Padangtegal.

Ubud Art Market

There are many places to shop for Balinese souvenirs. The famous ones is the Sukawati art market. Furthermore, the film Eat, Pray, Love, plays an important role in promoting the Ubud art market. Many tourists are interested in visiting the Ubud art market.

Bali Zoo Park

This small island, Bali, is not only has beautiful views of the coast, temples, lakes, valleys and mountains. But the zoo in the midst of extensive gardens and tropical trees become a special place for tours and fills your holiday activities with family, especially children. Bali Zoo Park is one of the largest zoos in Bali. Its existence is a target for tour destinations on the island of the Gods

Bali Bird Park

Bali is always offering an interesting destination to visit. One of them is Bali Bird Park, the one and only Bird Park in Bali which is located in Singapadu Village, has become one of the favorite destinations for tourists who vacation in Bali. Especially if you are a bird lover, but this tourist spot is not only visited by bird lovers, many tourists also come with their families and children to see and interact with the various types of birds found in Bali Bird Park.

Batik & Silver Shop

On the way to Ubud, you will pass through the area which is very famous with its Balinese hand-drawn Batik. In here, you will see the craftsmen doing their routine activities in making batik, both written and woven batik. So, if you interested in buying batik here, you can take some batik collections which is available. Next, we move to visit Celuk area that is well-known for its silver and gold crafts. You will see the craftsmen in the process of making Balinese gold and silver crafts.
