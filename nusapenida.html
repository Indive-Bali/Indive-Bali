EXPLORE NUSA PENIDA TOUR

If you are not satisfied only to visit east or west Nusa Penida, the you can combined it to reach all destination that you want to visit.

As Nusa Penida is famous with its beaches and snorkeling activities, then don’t miss your chance to experience the beauty of Nusa Penida Island.

Snorkling Spot

There are 3 famous spot for snorkeling that you can choose, namely Manta Bay, Gamat Bay, and GT Point. But, it is good if you ask first which one is available to snorkel due to sea condition. Meet colorful fish and see the beautiful of coral when you go snorkeling.

Angel's Billabong

This destination is a beautiful puddle of water. Angel’s Billabong looks like a seawater pool and its shape is very unique and naturally located between cliffs. Try to swim in the pool and make your vacation even more enjoyable. It is advisable to be careful, because the sharp rocks can make your feet hurt. If the tide is high, then you can’t be in this pool.

Broken Beach

Broken Beach is a beautiful beach formation on the southwest edge of Nusa Penida. You can watch the wave crashing from the Indian Ocean and if you are lucky enough, you can spot the silhouette of a giant manta near the surface of the deep blue water. Broken beach comes with a unique, serene and great views, which is a great place for photographers.

Klingking Beach

Kelingking beach is one of hidden beach which is located in Bunga Mekar village. This unique formation resembles the shape of the head of a T-rex, so Kelingking is often called the T-rex Bay of Bali. In this place, you will experience an extraordinary hidden beach framed by towering steep cliffs, deep turquoise water embraced by fine white sand with the most stunning views like heaven on earth.

Atuh Beach

With a stretch of white sand that adorns the coastline and combined with high cliff on either side of the beach also with stunning views, Atuh beach has its own style of beauty. On the east coast, there are several hills that make panoramic views from the highest cliffs. The biggest hill is called Padasan Bukit which perfect for fishing and camping. You can track, swim, and snorkel an exotic coral reef and colorful fish in this beach.

Diamond Beach

This beach is right next to the Atuh Beach and has hundreds of stairs. Diamond beach has a blue and clean water ocean, two coconut trees that are used to support swings surrounded by high cliffs. The white sand combined with high cliffs often makes you feel at home while enjoying the nature and bathing in the hot sun. Diamond beach has a lot of photo spots that you can try.

Tree House Nusa Penida

The tree house is located at an altitude overlooking Atuh Beach. The existence of this destination is quite extreme and challenging. From this place, you can see a spectacular viewpoint with panoramic views of the sea and various islands.
